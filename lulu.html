Lulu's Boba

I am currently working on creating a new drink's menu for Lulu's Noodles, an Asian restaurant in Pittsburgh. Designing a menu for Lulu's was out of my design comfort zone. I tend to utilize monochromatic and simpler color schemes for my past projects.I had a chance to work with the owners through various iterations from a simple layout to the actual menu. What is left for this project are promotional posters to accompany the visual changes to the menu and a digital version for their electronic displays;

International Justice Mission

IJM is a non-profit human rights organization that operates all over the world to rescue victims of human rights abuse. I believe that design should be used to solve more than just first-world problems. Being a part of CMU's chapter of IJM, I really enjoyed contributing to the cause with design by creating promotional posters for fundraisers and awareness campaigns.

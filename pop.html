POP Analytics

POP, which stands for Prototyping on Paper, is a software product that creates highly interactive prototypes from mockups and sketch. It is created by Woomoo Inc., a startup based in Taipei. Over the summer of 2014, I researched and curated a list of key performance indicators that were relevant to the team as well as investors. Then, I designed a dashboard that could display the metrics visually. I had to create an overview display of all the metrics at once as well as indepth displays for each metric. Throughout this project, I worked with the founder and engineers just as much if not more than I did with designers. I learned to evaluate my own designs from the perspectives of businessmen and engineers and collaborate with others in a work setting.
